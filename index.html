Outdoors is where life happens!

Join our hiking social club now and we will see you on the trails!

Get out of the city and see some of the most stunning scenery that the country has to offer with Pirin Hiking & Wild Camping Club. Weekend Hikes. Escape Sofia and see the sites of Pirin! We run hikes most Saturday and Sundays during term time, which take place in Pirin and Rila mountains. Members will be told about the weeks hikes via an email every Wednesday evening. Sign ups take place from the following Thursday 7pm. Once you have signed up you will be emailed the day before the hike with further details on where to meet us. Hike meeting places will be available using public transport. Our hikes are of varying difficulty meaning that we try to accommodate everyone from the mountain goats to those that are fearful of a set of stairs!

Spectacular views guaranteed!

Mission Statement:

Our hiking social club aims to bring together individuals who share a passion for the great outdoors and enjoy exploring nature through hiking. We strive to create a welcoming community that encourages physical activity, adventure, and social connections.

Membership:

Membership is open to individuals of all ages and backgrounds who are interested in hiking and outdoor activities. We welcome both experienced hikers and beginners. Our club organizes regular hikes in various scenic locations, ranging from easy to challenging difficulty levels. We also offer social events, such as picnics, camping trips, and community service projects.
